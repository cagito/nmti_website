## 이미지 목적

타임라인 **Trigger Event** 지점 — **조밀 sampling lines** · dynamic vectors → **warning dashboard** · detail **sharp impulse/spike waveform**.

## 필수 포함

- Timeline with explicit **"Trigger Event"** label (blast · threshold exceed · operator command)
- **Dense packed sampling lines** after trigger (high-frequency continuous mode)
- Signal wave lines → **warning dashboard** (time-series spike view)
- Detail window: **sharp impulse waveform plot**
- Clean **industrial logic chart** · white background

## 금지 (치명)

- lightning bolt decoration · explosion CG · action movie effects
- brain · hologram · abstract cyber
- normal-mode flat trend only (must contrast spike)

## Negative Prompt

lightning bolt hero, explosion CGI, brain, hologram, cyber matrix, flat trend only, soil cross-section, 3D render
